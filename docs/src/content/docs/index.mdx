## What is ChronoStar?

ChronoStar is a suite of smart contracts for time-based payment primitives on the Stellar network.

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="ScheduleVault" icon="lock">
    Lock tokens until a future ledger. Perfect for escrow, vesting, and conditional releases.
  </Card>
  <Card title="RecurringStream" icon="arrow-repeat">
    Stream payments continuously over time. Ideal for salaries, subscriptions, and dividends.
  </Card>
  <Card title="DCAPolicy" icon="graph-up">
    Dollar-cost average into any asset on a schedule. Automate your investment strategy.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>
